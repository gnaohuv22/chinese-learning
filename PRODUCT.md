# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Casual HSK learners following a university-provided custom routine. They engage in short, focused learning sessions—practicing lessons, writing Hanzi, and taking mock tests to gauge their level.

## Product Purpose
A learning platform that simplifies the start of the Chinese learning journey. It exists to provide an encouraging, low-friction environment where students can follow structured routines and track their progress without feeling overwhelmed.

## Brand Personality
Friendly, Playful, and Minimal. The voice is encouraging and supportive, balancing minimalist focus with playful rewarding moments.

## Anti-references
- "Boring AI-generated" look: Generic, over-templated UI.
- Textbook/Government portal: Dry, text-heavy, or overly formal/academic styles.
- "Working with academic papers": High cognitive load or intimidating layouts.

## Design Principles
- **Encouragement through Feedback**: Use vibrant, playful transitions and rewards to celebrate progress without distracting from the learning task.
- **Minimalist Focus**: Keep the core learning interface clean and simple to reduce cognitive load during study.
- **Student-Centric Warmth**: Design for a university audience that appreciates creativity and friendliness over corporate or academic rigidity.
- **Short-Session Efficiency**: Optimize layouts and flows for quick bursts of learning and practice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Prioritize a clean, creative interface that remains readable and accessible to students of all levels.
